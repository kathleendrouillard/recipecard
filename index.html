Chicken Vegetable Stir Fry for One

My favourite comfort dish to make and eat is Chicken Vegetable Stir Fry. It's my favourite because it is very easy to make and it's so versatile you can constantly be changing the flavours and types of vegetables and meats; it can taste different every time you eat it. I like to make it for my family because we can each customize how we want our stir dry to taste. The recipe on this website is of the stirfry I like to make for myself to eat.

Ingredients

For Stir Fry

  • 1 Chicken Breast
  • 1 Cup Rice
  • 2 Tablespoons Olive Oil
  • 1 Clove Garlic
  • ½ White Onion
  • ¼ Red Pepper
  • ¼ Green Pepper
  • ¼ Cup Sliced Mushrooms
  • ¼ Snow Peas
  • ½ Broccoli
  • Salt and Pepper To Taste
  • 1 Teaspoon Red Chili Pepper Flakes (optional)

For Sauce

  • ½ Honey Garlic Sauce
  • ¼ Chicken Broth
  • 1 Tablespoon Soy Sauce
  • Salt and Pepper To Taste
  • 1 Teaspoon Red Chili Pepper Flakes (optional)

Directions

  1. Cook rice according to package directions. Set aside when finished on a low heat.
  2. Heat up pan on medium-high heat with oil
  3. Cook chicken until white all the way through
  4. Add onion and garlic to chicken. Cook until onions are almost translucent.
  5. Add peppers, mushrooms, snap peas and broccoli. Cook until vegetables are tender.
  6. Add salt, pepper and red chili flakes to taste.
  7. Reduce heat and add chicken broth.
  8. Stir in honey garlic sauce and soy sauce. Add salt, pepper and red chili pepper flakes to taste.
  9. On a plate, place chicken vegetable mixture over a bed of the precooked rice. Add extra sauce from pan if need be.
  10. Serve.
